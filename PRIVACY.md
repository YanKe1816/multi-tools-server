# Privacy Policy

## Overview
- This server provides MCP tools for agents/systems and is not an end-user product.
- Requests are processed statelessly and are not used for profiling or advertising.

## Data We Collect
- We do not collect user accounts or login data.
- We do not store request payloads as personal data.
- We do not require cookies for functionality.

## How Requests Are Handled
- Requests are processed transiently to produce tool outputs.
- No request bodies are persisted as part of normal operation.

## What We Do Not Do
- No tracking, analytics, or ads.
- No selling or sharing of personal data.
- No profiling or behavioral analysis.

## Security and Retention
- No persistent storage of request payloads.
- Operational logs may exist for reliability and debugging and should not include request bodies.
